Generate an Instagram Reel from newsletter content using the reel-generator skill.

Follow all steps in the SKILL.md file at .claude/skills/reel-generator/SKILL.md to:
1. Get newsletter content (from conversation, arguments, or ask user)
2. Extract 4 reel slides (hook, lead story details, second story, third story/takeaway)
3. Build Remotion props JSON with brand presets
4. Render MP4 via Remotion at /Users/hari/Projects/newsletter-reels/
5. Open the rendered video

The reel uses animated dark backgrounds, kinetic word-by-word typography, glassmorphism cards, news ticker, and particle effects.

After rendering, add trending audio in CapCut or Instagram before posting.

Arguments: $ARGUMENTS (optional — paste newsletter content directly, or leave empty to use content from the current conversation)
