# Perplexity Hits $100M ARR Without a Single Sales Rep

**What's new?**

**Startup News AI:** Perplexity [announced](https://blog.perplexity.ai/100m-arr) it has crossed $100M in annual recurring revenue, making it one of the fastest AI startups to reach the milestone — achieved entirely through product-led growth with zero enterprise sales team.

**What matters?**

- The company's **$100M ARR** comes just 18 months after its last funding round, a $73.6M Series B that valued the company at $520M — likely putting its current implied valuation well above $1B.
- Perplexity's growth validates the [product-led approach](https://techcrunch.com/perplexity-100m) for AI search, with 80% of revenue coming from individual subscribers rather than enterprise contracts.
- The milestone puts direct pressure on **Google and OpenAI**, both of which have launched competing AI search features but haven't disclosed comparable standalone revenue figures.

**Why it matters?**

Product-led growth reaching $100M ARR without sales reps signals a viable alternative to the enterprise-first playbook most AI startups follow. Investors watching this space should note that consumer willingness to pay for AI-native products is proving out faster than most models predicted.
