## The Shortlist

**NVIDIA** [released](https://nvidia.com/blog/nemotron-ultra) Nemotron-Ultra, a 253B parameter open-source reasoning model that surpasses DeepSeek R1 and Llama 4 Behemoth across key benchmarks.

**Runway** [launched](https://runway.com/gen4) Gen-4, its latest video generation model with improved temporal consistency and support for longer clips up to 60 seconds.

**EU regulators** [finalized](https://ec.europa.eu/ai-act-code) the AI Act's code of practice, giving companies 6 months to comply with new transparency and safety requirements.

**Mistral** [introduced](https://mistral.ai/medium-3) Medium 3, a mid-range model that outperforms GPT-4o mini on reasoning tasks while maintaining fast inference speeds.
