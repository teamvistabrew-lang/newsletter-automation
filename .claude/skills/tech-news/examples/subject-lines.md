# Subject Line Examples

Short, punchy, business-focused headlines for a tech professional audience.

**Performance insight:** The best-performing subject lines are 5 words or fewer, use possessive format ("X's Y"), and avoid numbers. Target 5 words.

- Meta's $40B reality check
- Apple's AI pivot finally arrives
- Tesla's robot army takes shape
- Microsoft's cloud computing crown
- Google Cloud turns profitable at last
- Nvidia's $10B China problem
- Samsung's 2nm chip breakthrough
- Amazon's healthcare AI gambit
- Spotify's AI DJ goes global
- Cloudflare blocks AI prompt attacks
- EU fines Meta $1.3B over data
- Apple Vision Pro hits enterprise
- Intel's foundry comeback plan

- TikTok's AI content moderation push
- Twitter/X turns cash-flow positive
- Zoom's pivot to AI-first platform
