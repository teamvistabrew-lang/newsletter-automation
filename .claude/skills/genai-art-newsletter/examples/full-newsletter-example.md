# Runway Launches Gen-4 With 60-Second Coherent Video Clips

**What's new?**

**GenAI Art:** Runway [launched Gen-4](https://runway.com/blog/gen-4), its latest video generation model capable of producing temporally consistent clips up to 60 seconds — a significant leap from the 4-second limit of its predecessor.

**What matters?**

- Gen-4 uses a new **multi-frame conditioning** architecture that maintains character consistency and scene coherence across much longer sequences than any previous Runway model.
- The model supports [text-to-video and image-to-video](https://runway.com/gen-4-features) workflows, letting creators use reference images to guide style, lighting, and composition throughout the clip.
- Early testers report that Gen-4 handles **camera motion** far more naturally, with smooth pans and zooms that previously required manual keyframing in traditional video tools.

**Why it matters?**

Longer, coherent AI video has been the missing piece for professional creative workflows. With 60-second clips and consistent characters, Gen-4 moves AI video from novelty demo to practical production tool.

# Midjourney v7 Finally Nails Hands and Text

**What's new?**

**GenAI Art:** Midjourney [released version 7](https://midjourney.com/blog/v7), delivering dramatically improved hand rendering, reliable text generation inside images, and photorealistic skin textures that close the gap with photography.

**What matters?**

- Hands — long the telltale sign of AI art — now render with **correct finger counts and natural poses** in the vast majority of generations, based on early community testing.
- Text generation inside images works reliably for [short phrases and signage](https://midjourney.com/v7-text), making it practical for mockups, social media graphics, and concept art with legible lettering.
- The new model introduces a **style coherence** slider that lets users lock a visual aesthetic across multiple generations, useful for building consistent brand assets or storyboards.

**Why it matters?**

Hands and text were the two biggest credibility gaps in AI-generated images. With v7 closing both, Midjourney is positioning itself as a serious tool for professional designers, not just a creative toy.

# Google Opens Veo 2 Video Generator to Public Beta

**What's new?**

**GenAI Art:** Google [opened Veo 2](https://deepmind.google/veo-2-beta) to public beta through Google Labs, making its video generation model available to anyone with a Google account for the first time.

**What matters?**

- Veo 2 generates **1080p video up to 30 seconds** with strong temporal consistency, and supports both text prompts and image-to-video starting frames.
- The public beta includes [built-in SynthID watermarking](https://deepmind.google/synthid) on all generated content, making it one of the first major video AI tools to ship with invisible provenance tracking by default.
- Google is offering **50 free generations per day** during the beta period, significantly more generous than competitors like Runway and Pika which meter credits tightly.

**Why it matters?**

Google's free-tier approach could rapidly expand who uses AI video — from professional creators to small businesses and educators. The SynthID integration also sets a precedent for responsible AI content labeling.
